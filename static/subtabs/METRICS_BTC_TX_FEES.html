Bitcoin Transaction Fees Across Multiple Time Frames

About Bitcoin Transaction Fees

This chart displays the average transaction fee rate in sat/vB, the unit miners use to compete for limited block space (≈ 1 million vBytes per block).

Higher fee rates typically lead to faster confirmation, while lower-fee transactions may remain in the mempool for extended periods. As a result,
the mempool is structurally dominated by low-fee transactions, which explains why the average fee shown on the Home tab appears significantly lower.
