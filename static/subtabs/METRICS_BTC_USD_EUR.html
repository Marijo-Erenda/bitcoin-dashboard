Bitcoin Price Chart in USD and EUR

About Bitcoin Price

This chart shows the Bitcoin reference price in USD and EUR over time. It is intended as a macro-level price context, not as a short-term trading tool.

Displaying both USD and EUR highlights Bitcoin’s behavior across different fiat systems and helps distinguish global price trends from currency-specific effects.

For deeper market analysis, price data should always be interpreted alongside network metrics such as hash rate, difficulty, transaction volume, and fees.
