Bitcoin Value vs Fiat Currencies

About Bitcoin Value vs Fiat

This chart analyzes Bitcoin’s long-term value relative to major fiat currencies. Instead of focusing on short-term price movements, it highlights structural trends in purchasing power across completed historical periods.

Switching between USD, EUR, and JPY allows comparison across different monetary systems, while year-based and halving-cycle views help isolate Bitcoin’s behavior within distinct macro phases.

The default logarithmic scale reflects Bitcoin’s exponential growth and enables meaningful comparison across more than a decade of data.
